## Executive Summary

The African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA) represents the world's largest free trade area by number of countries, creating a single market of **1.4 billion people** and a combined GDP of **$3.4 trillion**. However, a critical infrastructure gap threatens to undermine this historic opportunity: **the $100+ billion annual trade finance gap** that systematically excludes African SMEs from cross-border trade.

**EarnX Protocol directly addresses this gap** by leveraging blockchain technology to democratize access to trade finance, enabling SMEs to participate in the AfCFTA vision.
